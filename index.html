ğŸ¯ Purpose of the CASE

This CASE exists to define how institutional governance is designed by PublicLogic and implemented by Polymorphic without overlap, ambiguity, or informal risk transfer.

It establishes authority boundaries, record obligations, workflow constraints, and survivability requirements before implementation occurs.
