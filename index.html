30-Minute Orientation Path (Recommended First Pass)

Read these four documents in this exact order to understand what VAULT is, why it exists, its boundaries, and whether it applies:

  1. Read_First.md — What this packet is and how to use it (5–7 min)
  2. Audience_and_Use.md — Who this is for + escalation paths (5 min)
  3. What_Is_Out_of_Scope.md — Explicit exclusions & boundaries (5 min)
  4. CASE_Workspace.md — The root immutable CASE container (10–15 min)

After these, you will know the purpose, authority model, and critical constraints. Proceed based on your role.
